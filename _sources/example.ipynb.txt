    "We can even specify multiple subsets of colours to include.\n",
    "For example, if we had more colours, say {0, 1, 2, 3}, we might also consider the inclusion of the sub-filtration spanned by simplices whose colours lie in any subset among (say) {{0, 1}, {1, 3}, {2}}.\n",
